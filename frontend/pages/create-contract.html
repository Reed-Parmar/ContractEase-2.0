Service Agreement

This Agreement is officially entered into as of January 15, 2024, by and between the service provider and Acme Corp (hereinafter referred to as "Client").

1. Services & Scope

The provider agrees to deliver the services as outlined in the description above in a professional and timely manner.

2. Compensation & Payment

In consideration for the services provided, the Client agrees to pay the total amount of $5,000. Payment shall be due no later than February 15, 2024.

3. Limitation of Liability

In no event shall either party be liable to the other for any indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or exemplary damages arising out of or related to this Agreement.

4. Confidentiality

Both parties mutually agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all proprietary information, trade secrets, and non-public business affairs disclosed during the term of this engagement.

5. Term & Termination

This Agreement may be terminated by either party at any time upon thirty (30) days prior written notice. Upon termination, all outstanding payments for services rendered shall become immediately due.

6. Signatures

By electronically signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to be firmly bound by all terms and conditions set forth within this document.
