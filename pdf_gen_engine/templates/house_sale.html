AGREEMENT FOR SALE OF A HOUSE

{{ contract_title or "Agreement for Sale of House" }}

THIS AGREEMENT of sale made at {{ agreement_place }} on {{ agreement_date }}, between {{ vendor_name }}, resident of {{ vendor_residence }} hereinafter called the Vendor of the ONE PART and {{ purchaser_name }}, resident of {{ purchaser_residence }} hereinafter called the Purchaser of the OTHER PART.

WHEREAS the Vendor is absolutely seized and possessed of or well and sufficiently entitled to the house more fully described in the Schedule hereunder:

AND WHEREAS the Vendor has agreed to sell his house to the Purchaser on the terms and conditions hereafter set-forth.

NOW THIS AGREEMENT WITNESSETH AS FOLLOWS

(1) The Vendor shall sell and the Purchaser shall purchase that entire house more particularly described in the Schedule hereunder written at a price of {{ sale_price }} free from all encumbrances.

(2) The Purchaser has paid a sum of {{ earnest_money_amount }} as earnest money (the receipt of which sum, the Vendor hereby acknowledges) and the balance amount of consideration shall be paid at the time of execution of conveyance deed.

(3) The sale shall be completed within a period of {{ completion_period_months }} months from this date and it is hereby agreed that time is the essence of the contract.

(4) The Vendor shall submit the title deeds of the house in his possession or power to the Purchaser's advocate within one week from the date of this agreement for investigation of title and the Purchaser shall intimate about his advocate's report within a reasonable period mutually agreed by both parties after delivery of title deeds.

(5) If the Purchaser's advocate gives the report that the Vendor's title is not clear, the Vendor shall refund the earnest money, without interest, to the Purchaser within a reasonable period mutually agreed by both parties from intimation of such report. If the Vendor does not refund the earnest money within such time, the Vendor shall be liable for applicable interest up to the date of repayment of earnest money.

(6) The Vendor declares that the sale of the house shall be without encumbrances.

(7) The Vendor shall hand over vacant possession of the house on execution and registration of conveyance deed.

(8) If the Purchaser commits breach of this agreement, the Vendor shall be entitled to forfeit the earnest money and may resell the property to any person.

(9) If the Vendor commits breach of the agreement, he shall be liable to refund earnest money, received by him, and liquidated damages as may be determined under applicable law.

(10) The Vendor shall execute the conveyance deed in favour of the Purchaser or nominee on receipt of balance consideration.

(11) The Vendor shall obtain statutory clearances and permissions required for completion of sale at the Vendor's cost.

(12) Expenses for preparation of conveyance deed, stamp duty, registration charges and all other out-of-pocket expenses shall be borne by the Purchaser.
